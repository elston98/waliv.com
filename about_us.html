About Us

Who We Are?


Waliv Global Services established in year 2011 has been providing Maritime Administration and Fleet Services to international shipping companies and ship management companies around the globe. The concept of our services is unique, we are committed to provide complete freedom to shipping companies and ship management companies from their day to day admin work. We provide bespoke services to suit your company's requirements.
